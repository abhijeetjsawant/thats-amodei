THE GAME IN ONE PARAGRAPH
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
One endless Senate hearing (THE FILIBUSTER): his real, labeled quotes fly
at you as orbs — tap on the beat to punch them back as cited rebuttals.
Build the CASE meter with jabs, cash it on the gold ★ release. When his
LOBBY meter fills he counterattacks — parry the gavels or he gains a
structural advantage. Knock him down and the lobby re-funds him: more
health, denser charts, the waltz a key higher and always accelerating.
AMORE MODE plays your own copy of the song's final chorus, looping +8%
faster each time until it breaks you. Knockdowns are the score.
